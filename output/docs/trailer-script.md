# Notion 3.0 Trailer Script

**[Audio]** 
*A crisp, upbeat lo-fi electronic beat begins to play. The tempo is steady and productive.*

**[Visual]** 
*A frantic montage of overlapping windows, multiple chat apps, messy spreadsheets, and endless browser tabs. A cursor moves erratically.*

**[Voiceover (Warm, confident, and approachable)]**
"Work is... messy. Tools on top of tools, tabs on top of tabs. Your team's knowledge shouldn't be a scavenger hunt."

**[Visual]** 
*The chaotic screen is smoothly swiped away, revealing a pristine, blank, white Notion page. A simple cursor blinks.*

**[Voiceover]**
"Enter the all-new Notion."

**[Visual]** 
*Typing begins. A forward slash `/` is typed, bringing up the commands menu. The user quickly selects "To-do list", "Board view", and "Heading". The page populates instantly with beautiful, structured blocks.*

**[Voiceover]**
"We've re-engineered everything you love. Giving you more control, more power, and unprecedented clarity."

**[Visual]** 
*Fast-paced cuts matching the beat of the music:*
*Cut 1: Dragging and dropping a kanban card.*
*Cut 2: Opening a database item to reveal a full page of notes.*
*Cut 3: A timeline view of a product roadmap zooming out.*

**[Voiceover]**
"Build the workflow your team actually needs. From engineering roadmaps..." 

**[Visual]** 
*Cut to a complex but clean engineering roadmap database.*

**[Voiceover]**
"...to your company wiki." 

**[Visual]** 
*Cut to a beautifully formatted company home page with an icon and a cover image.*

**[Visual]** 
*The music shifts to a slightly more elevated, magical tone. A purple sparkle icon appears on the screen next to text text.*

**[Voiceover]**
"And now, introducing Notion AI. Your ultimate thinking partner, built right into where you already work."

**[Visual]** 
*The user highlights a messy paragraph of notes. They click "Ask AI" and select "Summarize". Instantly, a clean, formatted summary appears.*

**[Voiceover]**
"Draft updates, summarize meeting notes, and turn scattered thoughts into actionable plans. Instantly."

**[Visual]** 
*A montage of diverse teams collaborating: avatars popping up on blocks, cursors with names moving across the screen.*

**[Voiceover]**
"Connected workspaces. Limitless possibilities."

**[Visual]** 
*The screen fades to black. The classic Notion line-art logo resolves in the center.*

**[Voiceover]**
"Notion. For your life's work."

**[Visual]**
*Text appears below the logo: "Sign up for free at notion.so"*

**[Audio]** 
*The beat resolves on an upbeat, resolved note and fades out.*
